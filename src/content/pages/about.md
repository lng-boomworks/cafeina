story_body: |
  Caféina is your cozy nook in the heart of our community, and is now under the fresh and enthusiastic ownership of Callum and Millie. We're a family business through and through, with a deep-seated passion for delivering unparalleled service to both our cherished locals and the wonderful holiday makers who grace us with their presence.

  Before taking the reins, Millie worked here for years under the caring eye of Maria — the founder who opened Caféina's doors back in 2009 and who spent over a decade making this corner of La Marina feel like a second home to everyone who walked in.

  Our mission is simple: to add value to your visits and elevate the level of service. We're pouring the same care Maria brought into every pot of coffee, every plate of cake, and every quiet afternoon spent over a good conversation — and we're building on it with fresh ideas, new events, and a cocktail list we're genuinely proud of.

  Join us at Caféina, where every cup of coffee and slice of cake comes with a side of heart and history.
